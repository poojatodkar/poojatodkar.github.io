How Web Works?

From my experience of interviewing developers, I was surprised to know how many developers have only a very limited or even no understanding of how the DNS process actually works. So just thought it might be useful to give a quick, high-level overview of the process that is followed when a DNS resolution request is made.

Computers communicate with other devices using IP addresses to identify eachother on internet. Humans can't remember IPs, hence they use words like `google.com`, `facebook.com`, etc.

Domain Name System (DNS) brings the two together and gets you to your destination.
